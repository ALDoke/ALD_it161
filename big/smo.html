Social Media Optimization (SMO)

In the most basic sense, Social Media Optimization is a marketing strategy that uses social media platforms to expand not just a brand’s name but also their product reach and desired customer numbers. This is done via not just producing content or ads on a social media platform, but finding out what best performs for your company or product and repeating that success to optimize how that is received in the future. Predominantly this is done by optimizing your ability to be seen through search engines; by finding relevant tags and terms to trends that currently are circulating popular social media platforms at the time. In addition there is a measure of what the target audience is for your company or product. Depending on the desired audience, that can directly shift where you will be advertising and trying to reach said audience. For example Facebook and Twitter will have vastly different userbases, so a product or tactic that will work on one platform will not necessarily work on the other. Some tools are available to those that wish to optimize their social media presence. Most of the mentioned plugins are used to change the presentation of their page and make it easy to access; these kinds of plugins are usually found for hosting services that let you fully customize your page and can aid those that are not as technologically minded. Every aspect of social media optimization tactics is used to make your content reach a wider audience, a more specific audience, or ensure that when viewers reach your social media platform it is eye catching and easy to navigate.
